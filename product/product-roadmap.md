# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Core Tracking & Progress

The foundation of the app - habit tracking, journaling, and progress viewing with seamless daily data entry. Users can log habits, track consistency, maintain streaks, journal their reflections, and view their progress through form or spreadsheet views with multi-modal input support.

### 2. Onboarding

Personalized onboarding flow that sets up each user's complete habit tracking and journaling system without manual configuration. Users finish onboarding with their habits defined, journaling format configured, and can immediately start logging data with no empty states.

### 3. AI Voice

Voice-first AI interaction system that enables hands-free logging, coaching, and natural conversation across all features, applied to each section where voice interaction is relevant.

### 4. Focus & Accountability

Aggressive blocking mechanisms and accountability features that enforce focus time, block distracting apps and websites, and help users maintain consistency through proactive intervention.

### 5. Analytics & Reporting

Advanced data visualization, natural language query interface, visual trend charts, goal setting and tracking, and comprehensive reporting tools to understand patterns and progress over time.

### 6. Screen Time Integration

Import and analyze screen time data from iOS Screen Time and Android Digital Wellbeing to understand digital habits and their correlation with personal growth metrics.

### 7. Wearable Integration

Connect and import data from fitness trackers and wearables (Oura Ring, Apple Watch, Fitbit, Garmin, etc.) to provide holistic health insights and correlate wearable metrics with habit performance.

### 8. Data Import & Historical Analysis

Import data from previous habit trackers (CSV, JSON, bulk import), perform AI-based trend analysis on historical data, and provide personalized recommendations based on patterns and trends.

### 9. Coaching Platform

Complete B2B coaching platform that enables coaches to manage their entire practice within the app. Coaches can create and deliver content, assign tasks and exercises, track client progress, and view all client data through intuitive overview dashboards that provide comprehensive insights into what's happening with their clients.

### 10. Feedback Loop

Reinforcement and recovery systems that translate daily inputs into actionable feedback, including completion confirmation, streak and milestone cues, weekly reviews, and pattern-based nudges to help users stay engaged and adjust course.

### 11. Data Visibility

Clear, trust-building visibility into habits and reflections through dashboards, trend charts, comparisons, and exports so users can see progress, identify patterns, and make informed adjustments.
