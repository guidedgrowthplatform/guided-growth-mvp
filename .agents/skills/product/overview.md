# Guided Growth — Product Overview

A personal-development platform that combines habit tracking, journaling, and proactive intervention. The app acts as both supportive companion and accountability partner — using AI insights and (later) blocking mechanisms to help users build lasting change.

## Problems We Solve

**Lack of consistency.** Without structure, good intentions fade. The app addresses this with habit tracking, streak monitoring, and (later) aggressive blocking to enforce focus and break boundaries.

**Lack of presence.** Days blur without intentional reflection. The app addresses this with journaling that creates space for mindfulness, plus AI-powered insights to surface patterns.

## Key Features

- Habit tracking with streaks and consistency monitoring
- Daily journaling for reflection
- Multi-platform (Chrome extension + mobile, real-time sync)
- Wearable + screen-time data integration
- App and website blocking with custom schedules
- AI insights (trends, patterns, suggestions)
- Voice-first AI interaction for hands-free logging and coaching
